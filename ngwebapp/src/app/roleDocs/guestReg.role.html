Night To Shine - Team Lead Site

Roles - Guest Registration

Team Leader: Mickey Rodriguez

Guest Registration Details

Guest Service volunteers will greet guests upon arrival, checking them in Airtable, and finding their pre-printed lanyard card which should be given to the buddy. Once check-in is complete, volunteers will assist in various areas as needed. At the end of the evening, the Guest Services team will help with the check-out process alongside the coat check and favors team.

Day of the Event

Check-in: 3:00PM

Event Location: Liquid Church, 299 Webro Rd. Parsippany NJ 07054

OFF-SITE Parking: There will be a shuttle bus to and from the off-site parking lots. The shuttle bus will drop you off at the volunteer entrance. Please allow extra time to park and ride the shuttle to the Webro building. Note: Parking at the Webro building is for event guests only.

  • Address: 200 Kimball Drive, Parsippany NJ 07054
  • Shuttle Times: 2:30pm - 11:00pm

Attire: All black attire. We will provide you with a Night to Shine t-shirt. If you would like to assist as a buddy, please dress in modest “Prom Attire” and comfortable shoes

Upon Arrival

  • Please make your way down the hall - once you have checked your coat and small personal items (coat check will be self serve) continue to volunteer check in.
  • NO CELL PHONES are allowed in the event, as per the Tim Tebow Foundation rules.
  • The Check-in team will then direct you straight to your volunteer huddle location where your team leader will be waiting for you.
  • Food will be provided throughout the night in the volunteer central.
  • “Many hands make light work”, at the end of the night gather with your team leader to assist in tear-down of your designated area.
